Our Company

Our History

Sadena Agencies Ltd has been a trusted supplier of quality Safety and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) in Kenya since 2007. As one of the leading importers, we provide head-to-toe protection solutions tailored to standard and custom needs. Committed to quality and customer satisfaction, we prioritize safety and reliability in every product.

Environmental Commitment

We are dedicated to sustainable practices in our operations and product sourcing. Our commitment extends to promoting eco-friendly safety solutions and reducing environmental impact through responsible business practices.

Public Safety

Our core focus remains on enhancing public safety through quality equipment, education, and support. We believe in creating safer workplaces through comprehensive safety solutions and expert guidance.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of safety solutions in East Africa, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and customer service while promoting a culture of safety consciousness.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive safety solutions that protect workers and businesses, delivering superior products and services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Associates

Our associates who are our manufacturers in South Africa, France and Italy are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with hands-on experience, thereby ensuring that our clients get the most effective and professional delivery and services.
